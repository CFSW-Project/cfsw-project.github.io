About OSP

**About OverSimplified** Welcome to OverSimplified! Our mission is to make CSS accessible and understandable for everyone. Whether you're just starting out or looking to refine your skills, we've got you covered. **Project OverSimplified** is designed to guide you through CSS from the basics to advanced concepts like Flexbox and CSS Grid. Our approach breaks down complex topics into easy-to-digest lessons, helping you grasp the full potential of CSS3. Explore our structured learning paths: - **Basics of CSS**: Start with foundational concepts and gradually build your skills. - **Flexbox**: Master the art of flexible layouts and create responsive designs with ease. - **CSS Grid**: Learn to build sophisticated grid-based layouts, enhancing your design capabilities. Dive into our tutorials and see how CSS can transform your web projects. Join us on this journey to simplify CSS and unlock its powerful features!
